    A Mini Bio

    Born in Cambodia, my family moved to Auckland, New Zealand when I was eight. [2012] I graduated university with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. [2014] I moved south to Invercargill to live with my partner and worked as a teaching assistant in the Information Technology department. [2016] Afterward we had new opportunities in Christchurch, so I moved there to work as a full time software developer. [2019] Recently arrived in London after a long distance bicycle tour from China to Portugal. After 21 months on the road, I’m settling in London for new career opportunities in IT.
